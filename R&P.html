REFUND POLICY

LOI — The Luxury of India

At LOI — The Luxury of India, we ensure premium quality products and a smooth customer experience. Please read our refund policy carefully.

Eligibility

Refunds apply only for damaged, defective, or incorrect items reported within the return window.

Non-Refundable Items

Sale items, limited drops, and used products are not eligible for refunds.

Return Conditions

Items must be unused, unwashed, and returned in original packaging with tags intact.

Refund Process

Once received and inspected, refund approval will be communicated via email.

Refund Timeline

Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days.

Shipping Charges

Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the error is from our side.

Exchanges

Exchanges are available for size or defective items based on availability.

Order Cancellation

Orders can only be cancelled before dispatch.
